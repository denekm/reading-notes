# Reading 01: Learning Markdown
## Different syntax
- ** ** or __ __ is used to make the text bold
- *** *** to make the text all bold and italic
- ">" Used to quote a text 

## Heading Sizes is determined by the number of hashtages
- "#" The largest Heading
- "##" Second largest heading
